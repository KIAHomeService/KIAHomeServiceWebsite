How our pricing works

Set price services

These use fixed prices that match your Square checkout links:

  • Bronze / Silver / Gold detailing packages
  • Weekly sedan / SUV interior plans
  • Weekly window touch‑up
  • CleanView interior reset
  • UPC pet & kid cleanup (if you set a fixed price)

You’ll plug in your exact Square prices on each service page.

Quote‑based services

These depend on size, access, or condition:

  • Window washing (exterior / interior)
  • Pressure washing driveways & concrete
  • Custom bundles or multi‑service jobs

These pages will clearly say “Quote required” instead of a set price.
